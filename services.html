Parish Services & Sacraments

Please find below the requirements and details for parish services.

Baptism Requirements

  • Original & Photocopy of Marriage Contract (if married).
  • Certified True Copy of Birth Certificate from City Hall or PSA.
  • For Non-Parishioners: Letter of Permission from respective parish.
  • For children 2+ years: Certificate of No Record of Baptism.

Baptism Fees

  • Special (Tue-Sat): 1,200 (Baptism) | 100 (Cert) | 100/Sponsor
  • Regular (Sun): 400 (Baptism) | 100 (Cert) | 50/Sponsor

Seminar Schedule

Held on the same day as the baptism:

  • Tue-Sat: Negotiated schedule.
  • Sunday: 9:00 AM

Confirmation

  • Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • Baptismal Certificate

Contact OLF Office for inquiry and reservation.

Matrimony

  • 2 months preparation period.
  • Baptismal/Confirmation Cert (marked "For Marriage Purposes").
  • CENOMAR and Marriage License/Civil Contract.
  • 1-2x2 ID Picture & List of Sponsors.
