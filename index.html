Welcome to Our Lady of Fatima Parish-OAD Website!

The Our Lady of Fatima Parish-OAD Antipolo is a Roman Catholic and a Missionary Parish in the Diocese of Antipolo, under the care of the Augustinians or Ordo Augustiniensium Discalceatorum (OAD), dedicated to bringing God's goodness to His people in the spirit of humility.

VISION

A community of disciples of the Risen Christ Enlightened by the Word of God Nourished by the Eucharist and Committed to the stewardship of God’s creation Journeying towards the Kingdom of God with Mary, Our Lady of Antipolo.

MISSION

Form and train people in the work of spreading God’s Word to the Community. Strive to receive the Sacraments and be transformed for others; Give witness to the life and mission of the Church towards the realization of God’s Kingdom.
