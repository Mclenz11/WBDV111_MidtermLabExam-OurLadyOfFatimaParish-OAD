Our Lady of Fatima Parish-OAD Website 

Fatima Boys: Quijano, Mclenz G. - Bolanos, Jonas L. - Flores, Jastin Claude L. - Primo, Mark Louiejie P.
BSIT 1-Y2-1

The website is a promotion for the Our Lady of Fatima Parish-OAD in the hopes of encouraging the people to visit their parish and hopefully donate to the construction of the new Church that they are building.

We were able to use the button features for the about us and the different section for the Parish Navigation. We also used the anchor for the navigation of the different pages of the website. We are also able to use the grid layout to show the different information and picture of the Parish.
