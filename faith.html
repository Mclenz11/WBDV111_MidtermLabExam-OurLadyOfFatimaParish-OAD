Historical Background

On May 13, 1917, three shepherd children Lucia dos Santos and her cousins Francisco and Jacinta Marto said they saw a beautiful lady dressed in white while tending sheep in Cova da Iria, near Fatima.

The lady later identified herself as the "Lady of the Rosary," but became widely known as Our Lady of Fatima.

The Apparitions

Our Lady of Fatima Parish (OAD), Antipolo, honors the Blessed Virgin Mary under the title of Our Lady of Fatima, remembering the six apparitions that happened in Fatima, Portugal in 1917. The parish continues to spread the message of prayer, repentance, peace, and devotion to God through church activities, Mass celebrations, and Marian devotions for the faithful in Antipolo City. Encouraging parishioners to pray the Holy Rosary every day as a way of strengthening faith and asking for peace and guidance from God. Promoting repentance and conversion by inspiring people to confess their sins, renew their relationship with God, and live according to Christian teachings. Sharing the message of peace, unity, and love among families and communities through faith-centered programs and religious celebrations.

  • Prayer, especially the Rosary

The Miracle of the Sun

The Miracle of the Sun is one of the most famous events connected to the story of Our Lady of Fatima. On October 13, 1917, thousands of people gathered in Cova da Iria, Fatima, Portugal, after the three shepherd children announced that the Virgin Mary would perform a miracle. Witnesses reported seeing the sun appear to spin, change colors, and move across the sky before returning to its normal position. Many people believed this extraordinary event was a sign from God that confirmed the apparitions of the Blessed Virgin Mary. At Our Lady of Fatima Parish (OAD), Antipolo, the Miracle of the Sun is remembered as a symbol of strong faith, hope, and trust in God’s power. The event reminds believers that prayer, repentance, and devotion to God can strengthen people during difficult times. Through Mass celebrations, Marian devotions, and parish activities, the church continues to share the message of Our Lady of Fatima and inspire the faithful to live a Christ-centered life guided by peace, compassion, and unity.

The Three Secrets

The Three Secrets of Fatima are important messages given to the shepherd children that focus on prayer, sacrifice, repentance, and the future of humanity. The parish helps educate devotees about these messages through catechism, religious teachings, and Marian activities. A vision of hell that reminds believers about the consequences of sin and the importance of living a holy and faithful life. A prophecy about future wars and suffering that encourages humanity to return to God through prayer and repentance. A message about the suffering and persecution of the Church, inspiring Christians to remain faithful and courageous during difficult times.

Recognition

In 1930, after years of careful study and investigation, the Catholic Church officially declared the apparitions of Our Lady of Fatima worthy of belief. This recognition strengthened the devotion of millions of Catholics around the world and made Fatima one of the most important pilgrimage sites in the Catholic faith. Many believers visit Fatima to pray, seek healing, and deepen their relationship with God through the guidance of the Blessed Virgin Mary. Our Lady of Fatima Parish (OAD), Antipolo, continues to honor and promote the message of Our Lady of Fatima through religious celebrations, Marian devotions, community outreach programs, and spiritual activities for parishioners. The parish serves as a place where people can gather in prayer, attend Holy Mass, participate in church ministries, and strengthen their faith as a Christian community. The church also plays an important role in spreading the teachings of love, peace, repentance, and devotion to the Holy Rosary. Through its official parish activities and online presence, including its Facebook page, Our Lady of Fatima Parish (OAD) helps connect and guide the faithful by sharing church announcements, religious teachings, livestreamed Masses, and updates about community events and celebrations.

The parish continues the mission of spreading the message of Our Lady of Fatima by encouraging prayer, faith, and service to others within the community of Antipolo City. Devotion to the Holy Rosary by encouraging families and parish groups to pray together and strengthen their relationship with God. Faith and penance through participating in church activities, sacraments, charitable works, and spiritual formation programs. Peace and hope by helping build a caring and united Christian community guided by the teachings of Jesus Christ and the Blessed Virgin Mary.
